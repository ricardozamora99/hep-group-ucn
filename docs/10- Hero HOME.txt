HOME HERO CONCEPT — FINAL DESIGN (HEP GROUP)

---

## GENERAL IDEA

The Hero is not a slideshow.
It is a **progressive visual composition** that introduces the research areas of the group and ends in a unified identity.

The animation builds meaning step-by-step:

* From individual research domains
* To a final integrated view of the group

---

## SEQUENCE OVERVIEW

STEP 1 → Base (Particle Physics)
STEP 2 → Left panel (Cosmology)
STEP 3 → Right panel (Computational)
STEP 4 → Final composition (3 vertical panels)

---

## STEP 1 — BASE STATE

* Fullscreen background:
  Particle Physics (LHC / detector)

* Visible text:
  Title:
  UCN Particle Physics & Cosmology Group

  Subtitle:
  Probing the Fundamental Building Blocks of Matter

* Layout:
  Clean, centered or slightly left-aligned
  No panels yet

---

## STEP 2 — LEFT PANEL (COSMOLOGY)

* A panel slides in from the LEFT

* Background:
  Milky Way / deep space

* The panel partially covers the base layer

* Subtitle updates to:
  Exploring the Universe at the Largest Scales

* The title remains fixed

---

## STEP 3 — RIGHT PANEL (COMPUTATIONAL)

* A panel slides in from the RIGHT

* Background:
  Computational / data / network visualization

* Now the screen has layered composition:
  left + base + right

* Subtitle updates to:
  Advancing Data-Driven Methods in High Energy Physics

* Title remains fixed

---

## STEP 4 — FINAL STATE (KEY TRANSFORMATION)

* The layout reorganizes into 3 vertical panels:

  | Cosmology | Particle Physics | Computational |

* Each panel occupies equal width

* Full height (100vh)

* Edge-to-edge (no gaps, no borders)

* Backgrounds:
  Left → Milky Way
  Center → LHC
  Right → Computational

---

## FINAL TEXT (CENTER)

* Only one text element remains:

  Particle Physics and Cosmology at UCN

* No subtitle

* No extra UI

* Clean, strong identity

---

## ANIMATION BEHAVIOR

* Transitions must be:
  Slow, smooth, cinematic

* Suggested timing:
  Step 1 → 2 : ~2s delay + 1.5s transition
  Step 2 → 3 : ~2s delay + 1.5s transition
  Step 3 → 4 : ~2s delay + 1.5s transformation

* Easing:
  ease-in-out (no linear motion)

---

## VISUAL STYLE RULES

* Panels are NOT cards

* No borders, no shadows

* They behave as full-screen slices

* All layers must have:
  Dark overlay (navy → black gradient)

* Consistent tone across all backgrounds

* Slight zoom/parallax allowed (very subtle)

---

## TEXT BEHAVIOR

* Title:
  Always fixed during steps 1–3

* Subtitle:
  Changes at each step

* Final state:
  Subtitle disappears
  Only central identity text remains

---

## INTERACTION / LOOP

Preferred behavior:

* Animation runs once
* Final 3-panel state stays visible

(Optional future enhancement)

* Reset loop after delay
* Hover interactions on panels

---

## CONCEPTUAL MEANING

The Hero represents:

* Particle Physics → fundamental scale
* Cosmology → largest scale
* Computation → methodology

Final state represents:

→ Integration of all domains
→ Identity of the HEP group at UCN

---

## DESIGN GOAL

The Hero should feel like:

“Entering a research environment that spans scales of the universe”

NOT:

“A typical website banner or slider”
